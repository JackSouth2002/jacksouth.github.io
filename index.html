ABOUT ME

  I am a first-year student at the University of Texas at Austin majoring in Corporate Finance and hoping to pick up a minor at a later date! My favorite TV show is Community, though some honorable mentions go out to The Office and Pair of Kings (best show of my childhood). As an Austin Native, my favorite places in Austin so far are Barton Springs, Zilker Park, and the Hill of Life on the Greenbelt trail.
